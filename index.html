About us

The club aims to raise awareness around diversity issues and to inspire students to address and try to solve any problem related to them. Through various activities the club attempts to make issues of diversity accessible, meaningful and fun. Above all the main purpose of this club is to bring students with different backgrounds, characteristics, stories, personalities and goals together to form a community that would value their differences and to create an environment of inclusion open to everyone.


As students arrive to ACG Deree, we imagine that the question of diversity carries with it a more specific set of concerns. How diverse is Greece, and how inclusive is it with its minorities? What is the experience of people of colour? How prominent is social exclusion?


Greece, and especially the city of Athens, has been undergoing frequent social changes over the years regarding these issues. Even just in the past few years we’ve seen dramatic changes, especially with the migration influx Greece has been facing. You’ll find that these topics are very much part of a public conversation, and that there is a vast amount of activism currently taking place towards pushing these conversations forward.


Our club, Diversity and Inclusion, aims to push those conversations on a campus level as well.


Join our club for activities that involve getting introduced to the Greek culture, minorities, and any other social topics. We would love to hear about your own experiences with those issues!
